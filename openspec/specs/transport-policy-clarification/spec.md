## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Transportation Policy Disclaimer in Form
The `Form` section SHALL display a disclaimer note immediately following the "Transportation Options" subtitle to clarify the complimentary dates.

#### Scenario: View transport disclaimer in form
- **WHEN** a user scrolls to the "Transportation Options" section
- **THEN** they see a note stating "*Complimentary transportation only applies to main arrivals on December 17th and main departures on December 20th."
