## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Update Wedding Dates in Hero
The `Hero` section SHALL display the updated wedding date range of "December 17 - 20, 2026".

#### Scenario: View updated wedding dates
- **WHEN** a user visits the landing page
- **THEN** they see "December 17 - 20, 2026" prominently displayed in the Hero section

### Requirement: Update Complimentary Transportation Schedule
The `Hero` section SHALL list the specific dates for complimentary transportation as December 17th, 18th, 19th, and 20th.

#### Scenario: View updated transport schedule
- **WHEN** a user reads the complimentary transportation description
- **THEN** the text includes "traveling on December 17th, on December 18th, on December 19th, and on December 20th"

### Requirement: Update LICENSE Year
The `LICENSE` file SHALL be updated to reflect the year 2026.

#### Scenario: Verify LICENSE year
- **WHEN** the `LICENSE` file is inspected
- **THEN** it contains "Copyright (c) 2026 Dari Developer"
